Working Hours & Conditions

  • Standard: 9–10 hours/day; overtime during deadlines.
  • Office-based: Comfortable, AC environments; field-based: outdoor exposure to weather and hazards.
  • Lab-based: Handle chemicals, electronics—strict safety protocols required.

Teamwork vs Independent Work

Most roles blend teamwork (project planning, design reviews) and independent tasks (coding, calculations). Leadership roles require coordination and people management.
