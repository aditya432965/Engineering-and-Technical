Employment Outlook & Demand

1. Current & Future Demand

  • Strong demand: Driven by AI, digital transformation, infrastructure and renewable energy
  • High-growth areas: AI/ML, Data Engineering, Renewable Energy & EVs

2. Growth Rate & Trends

  • Engineering roles expected to grow faster than average occupations.
  • Technological advances (AI, automation, cloud) shape new roles & require upskilling.
  • Tier-2/Tier-3 cities are emerging as strong alternatives to big hubs.

3. Technological & Social Changes

  • AI & automation require continuous learning.
  • Programs for gender diversity and reskilling are expanding.

4. Global vs National Opportunities

  • Global: High demand in US, Germany, Japan; remote work expands options.
  • National: Government initiatives (Make in India, Digital India) boost domestic roles.
